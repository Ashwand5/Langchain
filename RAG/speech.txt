Here are some of Thanos's most famous lines from the Marvel Cinematic Universe: 
"I am inevitable": Thanos's last words in Avengers: Endgame. 
"The hardest choices require the strongest will": A famous line from Avengers: Infinity War. 
"I know what it's like to lose. To feel so desperately that you're right, yet to fail nonetheless": A line from Avengers: Infinity War. 
"There are two more stones on Earth. Find them my children, bring them to me on Titan": A line from Avengers: Infinity War. 
"And that is destiny": A line from Avengers: Endgame where Thanos witnesses his 2019 self's death during the time heist.
